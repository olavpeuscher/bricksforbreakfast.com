The collective

Bricks for Breakfast is a recording collective, but foremost a band of friends. Formally playing in a coverband, where now this experience is put to use in producing our Originals from our living rooms. Style-wise, we prefer to mix it up a little. From electronic to 60s rock and everything in between. Every EP or album will have its own vibe.
