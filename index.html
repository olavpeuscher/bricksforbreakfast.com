About

Bricksforbreakfast is a Dutch recording collective formed in 2015, but foremost a group of friends. Formally playing in a coverband, where now this experience is put to use in producing our Originals from our home studios and living rooms.

Style-wise, we prefer to mix it up a little. From electronic to 60s rock and everything in between. Trying to mix some old sounds and vintage gear with new electronic influences. Our aim is to create every EP or album with its own vibe.

The people behind Bricksforbreakfast are Daan Wirtz (Guitar, Production, Vocals), Sander Clahsen (Drums), Thomas Husken (Bass), Joost Maas (Guitar, Lyrics, Vocals), Leon Melissant (Guitar, Production, Vocals), Olav Peuscher (Keys, Production), Xander Peuscher (Keys) and Yolanda Koevoets(Bitchin' vocals, Lyrics)

All musicians in Bricksforbreakfast previously played in coverband Tune Inn with 60 live performances and great gigs to look back ro.
